[LFX Insights](https://insights.linuxfoundation.org/) provides automated measurement and reporting of a variety of project metrics, including Baseline controls. LFX Insights covers over 10k critical open source projects that are not part of the Linux Foundation already.

If your project is not already included in LFX Insights, you can request to add it:

1. [Submit your project](https://github.com/linuxfoundation/insights/discussions/categories/project-onboardings?discussions_q=is:open+category:%22Project+onboardings%22+sort:top) to Insights’ discussion board
2. Ask your community to vote up your submissions –new onboardings are prioritized based on community upvotes

The Insights team reviews new submissions regularly.
